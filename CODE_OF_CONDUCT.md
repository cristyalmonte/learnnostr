# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Learn Nostr is committed to providing an inclusive, respectful, and productive environment for all participants. We welcome contributions from individuals regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, nationality, religion, political opinion, or any other personal characteristic. Participation in this project is based solely on the merit of constructive engagement, technical contribution, and alignment with open-source principles.

## Standards of Conduct

### Respectful Engagement

All contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to:

- Communicate professionally and courteously in all project interactions
- Provide constructive feedback and accept it graciously
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Focus discussions on technical merit and educational value
- Acknowledge and learn from mistakes

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated within the Learn Nostr community:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Threats of violence or advocacy of violence
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling, spam, or deliberate disruption of discussions
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe or unwelcoming environment

## Project Principles

Learn Nostr is an educational initiative dedicated to teaching the Nostr protocol and censorship-resistant communication infrastructure. Our focus is on:

- Open-source software development and education
- Technical excellence and accuracy
- Freedom of expression and information
- Decentralized communication technologies
- Transparent and collaborative development

This project does not engage in partisan political advocacy. Our commitment to censorship resistance and open protocols is a technical and philosophical foundation, not a political position.

## Collaboration and Contributions

### Contributor Expectations

All contributors must:

- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Respect intellectual property rights and licensing terms
- Provide accurate information and properly attribute sources
- Engage in good faith with the project's educational mission
- Follow technical guidelines and contribution standards

### Partner and Collaborator Review

Learn Nostr reviews potential collaborators, funding sources, and major contributors to ensure alignment with:

- Open-source software principles
- Educational integrity and accuracy
- Respect for freedom of expression
- Opposition to censorship and authoritarian control of information
- Commitment to decentralized and permissionless technology

This review process helps maintain the project's independence and ensures that partnerships support rather than compromise our educational mission.

## Enforcement

### Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers. All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- Warning and request for behavioral correction
- Temporary suspension of contribution privileges
- Permanent removal from the project and community spaces
- Reporting to relevant platforms or authorities when appropriate

The project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, commits, issues, and other interactions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and issue trackers
- Documentation and educational materials
- Communication channels (Discord, forums, etc.)
- Project events and presentations
- Public representation of the project

It also applies when individuals are representing the project in public spaces.

## Attribution and Modifications

This Code of Conduct is adapted from recognized open-source community standards and tailored to the specific needs of the Learn Nostr project. It may be updated periodically to reflect the evolving needs of the community.

For questions or clarifications regarding this Code of Conduct, please open an issue or contact the project maintainers directly.
